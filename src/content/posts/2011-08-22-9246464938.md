description: "A decommissioned industrial chimney or water tower dominates the urban landscape, its cylindrical form transformed into a monumental street art canvas. Vivid red paint forms bold concentric circles framing a intricate black mandala medallion at the apex, at whose centre a white dove in flight symbolises peace. Weathered graffiti text and blue spray-paint marks scar the lower sections, layering contemporary urban grit against the graphic clarity of the original composition. Shot against a brilliant deep blue sky under bright natural light, the contrast is stark and compelling—a meditation on urban renewal, activism, and the tension between preservation and decay. The surrounding brick tenement buildings anchor the composition in a working-class neighbourhood aesthetic."

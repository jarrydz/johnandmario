image_alt: "Fifteen pencil sketch portraits of men's hairstyles arranged in a grid, labelled with dates and location names, titled 'Twin Shadow Clean Cuts Tour'."
description: "A meticulously rendered graphite-pencil grid of fifteen male head studies, each capturing a distinct haircut in profile or three-quarter view. Drawn with precise hatching and tonal control on white paper, the portraits convey architectural precision in their rendering of volume, texture, and facial structure—from slicked-back and pompadour to cropped and textured styles. The composition is systematic and documentary in feeling, like a barbering reference chart or touring setlist visualised through portraiture. Cool, desaturated, the work has a coolly intellectual mid-2010s design sensibility: minimal text in a sans-serif typeface, institutional grid layout, and the austere beauty of black graphite on pale ground."

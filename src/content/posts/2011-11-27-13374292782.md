description: "A sculptural arrangement of reclaimed timber—three rough-hewn wooden planks lashed together in an angular, asymmetrical frame—sits on raw concrete. A rust-orange rubber ball nestles into the right corner of the wooden structure, whilst a smaller tan-brown basketball rests on the pale grey floor to the left. The wood is heavily weathered, stained with age and moss-green patches, creating stark textural contrast against the stark industrial setting and muted daylight. The composition feels playful yet austere, like a minimalist study in geometry, material decay, and curious spatial relationships."

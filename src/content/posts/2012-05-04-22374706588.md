The palette is ruthlessly monochromatic—pure white, pale grey, and deep charcoal—with only the warm, muted browns of book spines breaking the severity. The composition is deliberately austere and architectural, capturing the meditative emptiness of minimalist design with the warmth of accumulated knowledge."

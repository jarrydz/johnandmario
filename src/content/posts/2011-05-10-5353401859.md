description: "A vast, repetitive modernist apartment block in soft yellow-green rendered concrete, photographed in bright daylight with a cool, slightly desaturated palette. Each storey is punctuated by identical balconies wrapped in metal railings in clashing jewel tones—navy, burgundy, teal, purple, yellow—and crowned with white satellite dishes that create rhythmic white dots across the grid-like composition. The architectural severity is playfully disrupted by the accumulated personalisation of residents, evoking mid-century Eastern European urban photography with a contemporary, almost Pop-Art sensibility."

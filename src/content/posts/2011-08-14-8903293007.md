The warm amber light suffuses the austere architectural space with an ethereal, almost spiritual quality—a meditative contrast between cold geometry and ethereal illumination. The composition emphasises verticality and the play of light against weathered masonry, evoking contemporary installation art with a dreamlike, nocturnal mood."

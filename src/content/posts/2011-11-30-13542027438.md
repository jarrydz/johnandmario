description: "Sloped attic bedroom bathed in soft, warm daylight streaming through a large rectangular skylight, revealing a winter landscape beyond. The space combines natural wood beams and cream-coloured plasterboard walls with a stark white bedding arrangement on a low platform, adjacent to built-in shelving holding stacked books. A pale wooden ledge runs beneath the skylight, creating a reading nook, whilst white radiator panels and dark floorboards ground the minimalist Scandinavian aesthetic. The composition emphasises the geometric tension between the angled ceiling and the bright, almost ethereal quality of north-facing winter light."

Deep black washes drip vertically across the eyes and jaw, whilst warm rust and grey tones bleed through the cheeks and forehead, creating a raw, emotionally turbulent effect. The composition is frontal and close, with high contrast between the stark white ground and near-black gestures, evoking introspection and psychological intensity."

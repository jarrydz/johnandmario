description: "An imaginative sculptural bookshelf fashioned in the silhouette of an elephant, crafted from dark wood with white trim detailing, mounted flat against a pale grey-white wall. The interior cavities brim with books arranged spine-outward in jewel tones—deep crimsons, golden yellows, rich purples, teals and burnt oranges—their varied widths creating an organic, playful rhythm across the negatively-spaced form. Crisp gallery-style lighting casts subtle shadows, emphasising the three-dimensionality of the cutout design. The composition is centred and architectural, with a modernist sensibility tempered by whimsical, almost children's-book charm."

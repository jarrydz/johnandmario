A single human figure stands at the base for scale, emphasizing the monumental fragility of the construction. The light is diffuse and cool, typical of northern European coastal geography, casting subtle shadows that emphasise the raw materiality of stacked timber. The composition is stark and conceptual—a meditation on precarity, solitude, and human intervention in landscape."
